# ☕ Coffee Shop Sales Trend Analysis

## 📖 Project Overview

This project analyzes sales data from a coffee shop using **Microsoft Excel** to identify sales trends, evaluate business performance, and generate actionable insights. The analysis explores customer purchasing behavior, product performance, and revenue trends to support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

The objectives of this project are to:

- Analyze the overall sales performance of the coffee shop.
- Identify sales trends over time.
- Evaluate the performance of products and product categories.
- Measure key business performance indicators (KPIs).
- Provide recommendations for improving sales and profitability.

---

## 🛠 Tools & Techniques

- Microsoft Excel
- Pivot Tables
- Pivot Charts
- Slicers
- Excel Formulas
- Conditional Formatting
- Interactive Dashboard

---

# 📊 Research Questions and Methodology

## 1️⃣ What is the total revenue generated by the coffee shop?

### Method Used
- SUM aggregation using a Pivot Table
- KPI Card

### Purpose
To determine the total income generated during the analysis period.

### Dashboard Output
✅ Total Revenue KPI

---

## 2️⃣ How many customer transactions were completed?

### Method Used
- COUNT of Transaction IDs using a Pivot Table
- KPI Card

### Purpose
To measure customer purchase activity and transaction volume.

### Dashboard Output
✅ Total Transactions KPI

---

## 3️⃣ What is the total quantity of products sold?

### Method Used
- SUM of Quantity field using a Pivot Table
- KPI Card

### Purpose
To determine the overall number of products purchased by customers.

### Dashboard Output
✅ Total Quantity KPI

---

## 4️⃣ What is the average sales value?

### Method Used
- Average calculation using Pivot Table

### Purpose
To evaluate the average revenue generated over the analysis period.

### Dashboard Output
✅ Average Sales KPI

---

## 5️⃣ Which products generate the highest revenue?

### Method Used
- Pivot Table
- Sort (Descending)
- Bar Chart

### Purpose
To identify the highest-performing products based on revenue generated.

### Dashboard Output
📊 Product Performance Chart

---

## 6️⃣ Which product categories contribute the most revenue?

### Method Used
- Pivot Table
- Column Chart

### Purpose
To compare revenue generated across product categories.

### Dashboard Output
📊 Category Performance Chart

---

## 7️⃣ How do sales change over time?

### Method Used
- Pivot Table
- Date Grouping
- Line Chart

### Purpose
To identify monthly sales trends, seasonal patterns, and business growth.

### Dashboard Output
📈 Monthly Sales Trend Chart

---

## 8️⃣ What strategies can improve coffee shop sales?

### Method Used
Business Insight derived from the analysis.

### Recommendations

- Increase inventory of best-selling products.
- Introduce promotions for low-performing products.
- Bundle complementary products to increase customer spending.
- Focus marketing campaigns during slower sales periods.
- Monitor KPIs regularly for continuous improvement.
